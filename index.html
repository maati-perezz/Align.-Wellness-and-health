ALIGN. Wellness and health

Starting a healthy lifestyle takes time and consistency. Small habits can lead to big results. For example, limiting screen time improves sleep quality. Avoid blue light at least 30 minutes before bed and after waking up to support better rest and overall well-being.

Building strength, boosting energy, and improving mood all start with consistent movement. You don’t need hours at the gym ,short, focused routines make a big impact. Don’t forget to stretch and include mobility work to stay flexible and prevent injuries. Remember, rest days are just as important as training days.

Fresh juices and smoothies are a simple way to nourish your body with vitamins and hydration. Green juice helps detoxify and energize your body naturally. Beetroot juice supports blood flow and increases endurance, making it perfect before workouts. Ginger shots are great for your skin and immunity,they fight inflammation and give you a natural glow.

Eat balanced meals with colorful fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and protein. Prioritize mindfulness through gratitude, journaling, or meditation.Aim for 7–8 hours of sleep and take regular breaks from screens to recharge your mind. Progress takes time, be patient and celebrate every step.
