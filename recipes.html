RECIPES. wellness and health

Ginger shots recipe: Some pieces of ginger depending tolerance, 1 orange, 1 lemon (both peeled), 1 tsp turmeric, 1 & ½ cup of water, a pinch of black pepper, 1 red apple. Peele and chop all ingredients if needed, add them to a blender, blend. Finally, strain, add the pepper, place in jars and store. Drink 1 every day on a empty stomach

Green juice recipe: 1 cup spinach, ½ cucumber, 1 green apple, 1 celery stalk, juice of ½ lemon, ½ cup water or coconut water. Instructions:wash all ingredients thoroughly, then chop them into small pieces for easier blending, add everything to a blender and blend until smooth, finally, strain if you prefer a lighter texture. Drink every morning

Beetroot juice: 1 medium beetroot (peeled and chopped), 1 carrot, 1 apple, juice of ½ lemon, a small piece of ginger (optional), and ½ cup water.Blend all the ingredients until smooth, and strain if you prefer a lighter consistency. Enjoy before a workout for a natural stamina and energy boost. Drink every morning
